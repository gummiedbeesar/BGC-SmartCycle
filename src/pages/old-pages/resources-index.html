How To Use Mint

Toggling:

  1. Open the arrow in the right corner
  2. Once Mint opens, type "Hello"
  3. Ask Mint anything about recycling and waste!

Questions to Ask:

  1. Tell me about recycling
  2. What is a landfill?
  3. How do I stop wasting?
  4. How do I start recylcing?
  5. What happens if you don't recycle?
