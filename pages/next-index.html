What's Next?

As part of our hackathon solution, we used the IBM Watson Assistant to create a customized chatbot loaded with facts about recycling and waste. We then used IBM's Carbon Design System to help passust the frontend webpage.

In the future, we'd also like to incorporate other IBM Cloud services such as Language Translator, Speech to Text, and Text to Speech so that users may communicate with Mint in any way in any supported language.
